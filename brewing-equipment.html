Brewing Equipment

Discover our premium brewing equipment for coffee enthusiasts.

Espresso Machine

Price: $250

Usage Tips:
  • Use freshly ground coffee beans for the best flavor.
  • Tamp the coffee evenly to ensure proper extraction.
  • Preheat the machine before brewing to maintain consistency.
  • Clean the steam wand after every use to avoid milk buildup.
French Press

Price: $40

Usage Tips:
  • Use coarse ground coffee to prevent over-extraction.
  • Pour hot water (195-205°F) for optimal flavor.
  • Stir gently and steep for 4 minutes before pressing.
  • Press down slowly to avoid coffee grounds escaping.
Pour Over Kit

Price: $50

Usage Tips:
  • Use a gooseneck kettle for precise pouring control.
  • Wet the filter before brewing to eliminate paper taste.
  • Pour in slow circular motions to evenly extract coffee flavors.
  • Adjust grind size based on brew time (medium-fine grind works best).
